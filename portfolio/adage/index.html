Advertising Age
A news media publisher covering the advertising industry for millions of marketing professionals.


Background
Advertising Age is a news and media publication owned by Crain Communications. For 80 years Ad Age has covered advertising, marketing and media news for industry professionals, earning it countless awards and general industry recognition as "the bible of advertising." I served as Ad Age's first Digital Product Design Director for 5 years helping them grow their Internet revenue by 300%. I designed and managed development of more than a dozen products developing style guides to maintain consistent branding across Web and mobile platforms.
AdCritic.com Redesign
After the announcement of their acquisition of AdCritic.com (the most popular video site on the Web before YouTube), Ad Age recruited me to redesign the site to cater to professional users in the design and creative segment of the advertising industry. The new design needed to support a "freemium" business model combining subscription and advertising revenues. I worked closely with the editorial, marketing, sales, circulation and executive management teams of Crain Communications to introduce and manage a process for design and deployment. I began the process with my own research and design proposals. I questioned staff and users, documented my findings and produced wireframes to propose layout of content.
